About the Course

Levels Academy’s Sunday School English Course is a fun and engaging language program designed for children aged 6–10. Our carefully structured lessons help young learners build a strong foundation in English while making learning an enjoyable experience. Through interactive activities, storytelling, songs, and games, children develop essential language skills in a relaxed and supportive environment.

Our expert instructors use age-appropriate teaching techniques to encourage curiosity, confidence, and creativity in language learning. Whether your child is just starting or looking to improve their English, this course provides the perfect balance of education and fun!

Frequently Asked Questions

What will my child learn in this course?
Children will develop speaking, listening, reading, and writing skills through engaging lessons, games, and storytelling.
What materials will my child use?
Each student receives a Cambridge University Kids Box and Oxford University Family and Friends textbooks, along with additional engaging learning materials.
When does the course start?
New groups start regularly. Contact us to find out the next available start date and secure your child’s spot.
How do you form groups?
Groups are formed based on age and language proficiency to ensure the best learning environment for each child.
How many levels will my child complete?
The number of levels depends on progress and attendance. Most students advance two levels per academic year.
What is the lesson structure?
